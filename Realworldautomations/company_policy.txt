COMPANY POLICY DOCUMENT

Company Name: NovaTech Solutions Ltd.
Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

NovaTech Solutions Ltd. is committed to maintaining a professional, ethical, and productive work environment. This policy outlines the expectations, responsibilities, and standards required of all employees.

2. Code of Conduct

All employees are expected to:

Act with honesty and integrity at all times

Treat colleagues, clients, and partners with respect

Avoid any form of discrimination, harassment, or misconduct

Maintain professionalism both in and outside the workplace when representing the company

3. Attendance and Punctuality

Employees must adhere to assigned working hours

Absences must be reported to supervisors in advance where possible

Repeated lateness or absenteeism may result in disciplinary action

4. Dress Code

Employees are expected to dress in a neat and professional manner

Casual wear may be permitted on designated days

Clothing must be appropriate for a workplace environment

5. Use of Company Property

Company equipment must be used responsibly and for work-related purposes

Unauthorized use of company resources is prohibited

Employees are responsible for any damage caused through negligence

6. Confidentiality

Employees must not disclose sensitive company or client information

Confidential information includes business strategies, financial data, and client records

Breach of confidentiality may lead to termination

7. Internet and Technology Use

Company internet access should be used primarily for work purposes

Accessing inappropriate or harmful content is strictly prohibited

Employees must follow cybersecurity guidelines to protect company data
The Wi-Fi password is 'BlueOcean2026

8. Health and Safety

Employees must comply with all safety regulations

Unsafe conditions should be reported immediately

The company is committed to providing a safe working environment

9. Disciplinary Action

Failure to comply with company policies may result in:

Verbal warning

Written warning

Suspension

Termination of employment

10. Equal Opportunity Policy

NovaTech Solutions Ltd. provides equal employment opportunities to all individuals regardless of:

Gender

Age

Religion

Ethnicity

Disability

11. Leave Policy

Employees are entitled to annual leave as stated in their contract

Sick leave must be supported by valid medical documentation where required

Requests for leave should be submitted in advance

12. Policy Review

This policy may be reviewed and updated periodically. Employees will be notified of any changes.

13. Acknowledgment

All employees are required to read, understand, and comply with this policy.
